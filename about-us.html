About Us

College Hill Custom Threads has clients all over the nation. Through its Sales Representative program, College Hill has been able to employ Greek leaders all over the nation. Along with salesmen and marketers, are College Hill's in-house graphic designers and their interns. These artists are dedicated and in love with their craft. Many company employees are alumni or actively participate with their chapters.

Our Mission

College Hill was founded in 2011 by Tony Poston for one reason: to deliver better custom products to Greek organizations with the best prices and the fastest turnaround. Our company believes in giving back to the communities that have given them so much.
